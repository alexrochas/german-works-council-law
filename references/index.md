# Reference Index

This skill keeps the legal workflow small and puts the actual source material in `references/generated/`.

## Source Policy

- Primary source: official German text from `gesetze-im-internet.de`
- Working language: German source first, English only as a convenience when needed
- For high-stakes or date-sensitive matters, verify the relevant official online text before finalizing the answer

## Generated Statute Snapshots

- `generated/betrvg.md`
  Core works council statute. Start here for structure, powers, procedure, elections baseline, co-determination, personnel matters, and restructuring.
- `generated/wo-betrvg.md`
  Election regulation. Use for operational election steps, voter lists, notices, nominations, ballots, and counts.
- `generated/agg.md`
  Equal-treatment and discrimination overlap.
- `generated/arbzg.md`
  Working-time overlap, especially with `§ 87 Abs. 1 Nr. 2, 3 BetrVG`.
- `generated/arbschg.md`
  Health and safety overlap, especially with `§§ 80, 87, 89-91 BetrVG`.
- `generated/bdsg.md`
  Employee-data overlap, especially with `§ 79a BetrVG` and `§ 26 BDSG`.
- `generated/gdpr.md`
  Official GDPR text snapshot for article-level privacy overlap, especially principles, legal bases, special-category data, transparency, and DPIA issues.

## Hand-Written Guidance

- `topic-map.md`
  Quick map from issue type to the most relevant provisions.
- `privacy-workflows.md`
  Curated privacy-heavy workflows and the recommended first-pass authority bundles.
- `case-law-notes.md`
  Place for future case-law notes, commentary takeaways, or recurring internal interpretations. This file starts intentionally lean.

## Scope Notes

- GDPR is relevant in many works-council data questions. Use `generated/gdpr.md` together with `generated/bdsg.md` and `§ 79a BetrVG`.
- If a question turns on European data-protection provisions, current labor-court case law, sector-specific rules, or collective agreements, say so and verify beyond the bundled sources.
